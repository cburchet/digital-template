Raining Cats and Dogs

  • High Concept: Catch the dogs falling from the sky and avoid the cats.
  • Player Experience Goals: Fun as they chase the falling dogs and avoid the falling cats for points.
  • Genre: A casual scoring game
  • Game play: Use the arrow keys to move left, right, and jump. Catch the dogs before they disappear to get 10 points and don't catch the cats. They'll land on their feet anyway...
  • Prototype goal: Creating cats and dogs in the sky and collecting them for points.
  • Features: Cats and dogs spawn at random heights with random speeds to be caught.
  • Setting/Premise: As a dog catcher it is your duty to catch all the falling dogs before they hit the ground. Don't catch the cats they'll land on their feet anyway...
  • Story: NA
  • Target Audience: casual gamers, dog lovers, cat haters
  • Play time: 2 minutes
  • Strategies: Standing in the middle will allow the player the best options to chase down and catch the dogs before they disappear.
  • Prototype Assets: http://opengameart.org/
